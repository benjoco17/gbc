Easily create online payment forms with Gravity Forms and eWAY.

== Description ==

A flexible and powerful add-on that helps you build payment forms for your website, from simple donation forms to sophisticated forms with many options and conditions.

= Requirements =

* you need [Gravity Forms](https://webaware.com.au/get-gravity-forms) installed and activated
* you need an [eWAY account](https://eway.io/)
* a valid SSL/TLS certificate (even a free one) [is recommended](https://gfeway.webaware.net.au/faq/do-i-need-an-ssl-certificate/), but not required for one-off payments
